Speakify

About

Speakify is an AI-powered tool that converts PDFs into audio books, making content more accessible and versatile. It offers customizable voice options, the ability to generate QR codes from PDFs for easy sharing, and creates AI-generated question banks from PDF content, which is great for educational purposes. Speakify supports multiple languages, enhancing its global appeal.
